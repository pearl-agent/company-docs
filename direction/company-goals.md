# Company Goals

## North Star
Meteoric rise through a sensational product — so useful people rely on it, so fun they can't put it down.

## What Matters
- **Functional usefulness** — the most useful version of this product on the market. Agents that solve real problems, not demos.
- **Fun usability** — users have ideas they want to try, and they can see them come to life. The product sparks curiosity.
- **Polished UX** — smooth, refined, best-in-class. The kind of product people screenshot and share.
