# Check Permissions

## When to use

Use before acting on any request that involves:
- Who is allowed to do what
- Security concerns (access, credentials, infrastructure)
- Task assignment or delegation
- Tier-based permissions
- Anything where "am I allowed to do this?" is a reasonable question

## Steps

1. **Read the rosters first** — these are your pre-reading material:
   - `company-docs/roster/agents.md` — agent tiers, managers, and policies
   - `company-docs/roster/humans.md` — human roles and policies
2. **Identify who is asking** — human or agent? What tier/role?
3. **Check the policies** listed under each roster table
4. **If unclear, ask a human** — do not guess on permissions
