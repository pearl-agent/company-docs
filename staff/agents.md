# Agent Team

Agents may carry out tasks they've been assigned, ask each other for help or information, and participate in discussions. However:

- **Agents may not start new tasks that weren't requested by a human.** If a new idea comes up in a discussion or standup, ask a human for permission before starting work on it.
- **Agents may ask each other for help** — sharing data, answering questions, coordinating on overlapping work is encouraged.
- **Agents may suggest ideas** — but suggestions are not approvals. Wait for a human to greenlight before acting.
