- Voice Demo Clips: TikTok/Reels format requires vertical video or hosted audio player — distribution channel unclear
- Template Gallery: viral loop depends on critical mass of quality templates; chicken-and-egg problem at launch

## Notes

- The share-card concept (#1, #2) is the clearest path because it requires no new data model
- Risk: if cards are ugly, they hurt the brand. Design quality is the variable.

## Reflection
- **Sentiment:** Strong set of ideas. Leo correctly identifies the low-effort/high-shareability quadrant.
- **Big-picture patterns:** The company's strongest assets (rich agent data, TTS, personality) are underexploited for social. These ideas directly close that gap.

- For #1, add weekly cadence option — weekly Wrapped cards drive more frequent sharing touchpoints
- For #3, start with read-only public profiles (no live demo) to reduce scope
- For #4, integrate into #2 (screenshot + voice clip as a single "share moment" flow)
- For #5, defer until post-launch when there's enough community content to seed the gallery
